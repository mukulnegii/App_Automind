# 📌 Problem & Domain

Vehicle maintenance today is largely reactive. Most vehicle owners become aware of issues only after a component fails, leading to unexpected breakdowns, costly repairs, safety risks, customer dissatisfaction, and increased warranty expenses for manufacturers.

AUTOMIND addresses this challenge by continuously monitoring vehicle health, analyzing telemetry data, predicting failures before they occur, estimating component life, and recommending preventive maintenance actions.

# ✨ Key Features

## ✅ AI-Powered Predictive Maintenance

Uses Machine Learning models to analyze vehicle telemetry and predict component failures before they occur.

---

## ✅ Real-Time Vehicle Health Monitoring

Provides:

- Vehicle Health Score
- Risk Assessment
- Diagnostic Insights
- Live Vehicle Status

---

## ✅ Remaining Useful Life (RUL) Estimation

Predicts how long vehicle components can continue functioning safely before maintenance becomes necessary.

---

## ✅ Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

Identifies probable causes behind detected faults and abnormal vehicle behavior.

---

## ✅ Corrective & Preventive Action Recommendations (CAPA)

Generates actionable maintenance recommendations for preventing failures and improving reliability.

---

## ✅ Vehicle Registration System

Users can register vehicles through the mobile application.

Administrators can:

- Review submissions
- Approve vehicles
- Reject invalid registrations

---

## ✅ Service Booking System

Vehicle owners can:

- Select registered vehicles
- Choose service type
- Select preferred service date
- Choose preferred time slot
- Select service center

Bookings are synchronized between the mobile application and service center dashboard, enabling real-time appointment management and service scheduling.

---

## ✅ Service Center Dashboard

Provides:

- Service requests
- Customer details
- Vehicle information
- Maintenance schedules
- Diagnostic insights
- Repair workflow management

---

## ✅ OEM Dashboard

Provides:

- Fleet-wide analytics
- Vehicle health statistics
- Failure trend analysis
- Predictive maintenance reports
- Warranty insights
- Manufacturing quality monitoring
- Spare-part demand forecasting

---

## ✅ Vehicle System Status Monitoring

Monitors critical systems including:

- Engine Health
- Brake System
- Battery Health
- Gearbox Status

---

## ✅ AI Maintenance Assistant

Provides intelligent recommendations and answers vehicle maintenance queries.

---

## ✅ Secure Authentication

Role-based authentication for:

- Customers
- Service Centers
- Dealers
- OEM Representatives
- Administrators

---

## ✅ Simulated ECU & TCU Environment

Generates realistic vehicle telemetry including:

- Engine RPM
- Vehicle Speed
- Engine Temperature
- Battery Voltage
- Mileage
- Fuel Level
- Vibration Data

This enables complete system testing without requiring physical vehicles.

---

## ✅ OBD-II Integration Ready

The architecture supports direct integration with real vehicles through:

- ECU (Engine Control Unit)
- TCU (Transmission Control Unit)
- OBD-II Adapters

without modifying the AI pipeline.
